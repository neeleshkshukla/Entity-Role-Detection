Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar , who visited the Mahabodhi temple condemned the attack but maintained that security had been provided to the religious site . Any condemnation of this incident would not be enough . Bodh Gaya is a sacred pilgrimage site and the key location for the faith of millions across the world . To carry out such an attack at such a place in condemnable , he said . The government had always been on the alert and taken steps to ensure the security of the Mahabodhi temple and Bodh Gaya in general . . . RJD chief Lalu Prasad Yadav held the government responsible for failing to act upon intelligence inputs . . . LJP leader Ramvilas Paswan demanded Mr. Kumars resignation on moral grounds for failing to prevent the attack despite intelligence inputs .
