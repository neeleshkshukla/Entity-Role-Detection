Judicial Magistrate III ( in-charge ) Arunachalam on Saturday granted permission to a Special Investigation Team of the State Crime Branch CID to produce Kerala-based People 's Democratic Party ( PDP ) leader Abdul Nasser Maudhany on November 12 . The case relates to the planting of a bomb inside the telephone booth at the Coimbatore Press Club on December 30 , 2002 . Warrant The Special Investigation Team had obtained a production warrant from the Coimbatore Court against Mr. Maudhany and formally recorded his arrest in the Bangalore prison earlier . Mr. Maudhany is lodged in the Bangalore prison in connection with a Bangalore bomb blast case . Citing his health condition , Mr. Maudhany expressed his inability to travel . Hence the court ordered Mr. Maudhany to be produced on November 12 . OPEN
