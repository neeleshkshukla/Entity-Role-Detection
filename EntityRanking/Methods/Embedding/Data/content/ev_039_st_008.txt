Special Correspondent . . NEW DELHI : The Centre on Monday sought a report from the Assam government on the bomb blast in Guwahati . Union Home Secretary Madhukar Gupta , who spoke to the Assam Chief Secretary , advised him to strengthen security arrangements . . . Mr. Gupta assured all help to trace those involved in the explosion . . . The blast has evoked concern in the Home Ministry and the security establishment here . Sources said the worry was that elements related to the terror network could possibly use the occasion of the Lok Sabha poll campaign to strike at poll meetings .
