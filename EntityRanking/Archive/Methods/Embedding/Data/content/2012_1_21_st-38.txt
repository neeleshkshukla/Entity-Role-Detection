A court here on Friday extended the police custody of six men alleged to be members of the Indian Mujahideen and suspected of involvement in shooting at foreign tourists and triggering a bomb near Jama Masjid here just before the 2010 Commonwealth Games . Chief Metropolitan Magistrate Vinod Yadav extended their custody till February 4 . These men are also suspected of being involved in other terror cases including the Pune German Bakery blast and the Bangalore Chinnaswamy Stadium blast . A seventh suspect is presently in judicial custody at Tihar Jail . OPEN
