Security forces on Thursday gunned down the main accused in the serial bombings that rocked Guwahati on January 1 , officials said.A police spokesman said 20-year-old Pranjal Deka of the banned United Liberation Front of Assam ( ULFA ) terror outfit was killed in a gunbattle at Ghilakuti village near Rangiya town , about 70 km west of Guwahati . It was a joint Assam Police and army operation . There were about three of them , including Deka , a police official said . .
