Two militants of the National Democratic Front of Bodoland ( NDFB ) , belonging to the Ranjan Daimary-led anti-talks faction and suspected to be involved in two bomb blasts on rail tracks last week , surrendered to the police here in Sonitpur district on Wednesday . Birman Basumatari and Gagan Basumatari were allegedly involved in the blasts on the tracks near Batasipur in Sonitpur district and Mazbat in Udalguri district . The militants , who surrendered at the Missamari station , deposited two 9 mm pistols , three rounds of ammunition and a hand-grenade . PTI OPEN
