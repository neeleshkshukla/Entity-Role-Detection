View PhotosSibsagar , Sept 24 : Eight persons were injured , one of them seriously , when a blast rocked this town in Assam late Monday night . . . Police said the blast , suspected to be caused by an IED , was triggered between Central Market and Lakshmi talkies in the heart of the headquarters town of Sibsagar district at 9 pm . The injured were taken to Sibsagar Civil Hospital and another private hospital nearby . . . Glass panes of some nearby constructions were broken by the impact of the blast . . . .
