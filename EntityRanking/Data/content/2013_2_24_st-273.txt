One among the 16 persons who died in the twin bomb blasts that rocked Hyderabad on Thursday was from Karnataka , the police said here on Friday . The deceased has been identified as Padmakar Kulkarni ( 45 ) , a resident of Bidar , said Satish Kumar , Bidar Superintendent of Police . His charred remains were identified by some of his friends . The police said Mr. Kulkarni was a sweetmeat vendor at Dilkushnagar and had been living in Hyderabad for the last one year . PTI OPEN
