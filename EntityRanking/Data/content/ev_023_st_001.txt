Special Correspondent . . Guwahati : Five police personnel were killed in a bomb attack by the United Liberation Front of Asom in upper Assam on Friday . . . Inspector General of Police , Special Branch , Khagen Sharma told The Hindu that an improvised explosive device , planted by the ULFA , went off at Ratanipathar under Pengeri police station in Tinsukia district around 5.15 p.m. just after the vehicle of Tinsukia SP Debajit Hazarika had crossed the area . . . The blast ripped apart the vehicle of Sub-Divisional Police Officer of Margherita , which was closely following the SP 's vehicle .
