The Kodagu police acted on complaints by two persons one from Somwarpet and another from Siddapur in the district in registering cases against Tehelka reporter K.K. Shahina and others , Superintendent of Police Manjunath Annigeri told The Hindu here on Saturday . It is not a suo motu case , he clarified . Ms. Shahina and four others visited Hosathota in Somwarpet taluk last week , where K. Nasir , who was arrested in connection with the Bangalore bomb blasts case , stayed in the guise of a ginger cultivator until his disappearance months before the blasts in 2008 . Journalists in Kerala submitted a memorandum to their Chief Minister V.S. Achuthanandan expressing concern over the registering of cases . OPEN
