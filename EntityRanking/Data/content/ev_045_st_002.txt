One of the injured , 14-year-old Purnima Rojok , succumbed to her wounds at the Assam Medical College Hospital where the other injured were admitted , the sources said . . . Preliminary investigations pointed to a crude bomb and not a grenade as no splinters were found at the site of the blast and also there was no trace of an improvised explosive device -LRB- IED -RRB- , Mr. Bhuyan said . . .
