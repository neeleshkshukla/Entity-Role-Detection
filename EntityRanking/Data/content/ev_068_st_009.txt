Gorakhpur , May 22 : At least three bomb blasts were triggered in the heart of Gorakhpur District of Uttar Pradesh around 7.15 p.m. on Tuesday , injuring at least six persons . . . The first blast took place near Baldev Plaza , the second near Golghar Crossing and the third at Jalkal Building . . . According to reports , the bombs were kept inside lunch boxes in scooter and bicycle . . . The injured have been admitted in a nearby hospital . . . Additional District Magistrate P K Agarwal , however , denied any serious injuries . . .
