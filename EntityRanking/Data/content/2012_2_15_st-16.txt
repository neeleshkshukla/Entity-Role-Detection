Kamal Chauhan , a disgruntled RSS worker arrested by the National Investigation Agency , planted bombs onboard the Samjhauta Express in 2007 after undergoing training in arms and explosives in Haryana and Madhya Pradesh , the agency said on Tuesday . He was taken to the Panchkula court on Tuesday after his arrest in Noida on Sunday . The court remanded him in the agency 's custody till February 24 for questioning on his alleged role in the blast that killed 68 people , mostly Pakistanis . Investigations reveal that Chauhan along with Ramchandra Kalasangra alias Ramji and Lokesh Sharma , also charged in the case planted the explosives in the train at Old Delhi , from where the train originates , NIA 's Special Prosecutor R.K. Handa said outside the court . PTI OPEN
