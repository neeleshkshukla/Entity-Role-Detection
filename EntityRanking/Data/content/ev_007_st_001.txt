Special Corespondent . . Guwahati : Two persons were seriously injured in two blasts at Srirampur , a township on the Assam-West Bengal border , in Kokrajhar district on Friday . Police recovered a bomb in the locality . . . Police said the first explosion occurred around 10.30 a.m. under a truck that was parked near the inter-State check post in which a school teacher was injured . The second blast occurred an hour later , when a bomb kept in a bag on a bicycle exploded near a petrol pump . A truck driver was injured . . . Another bomb was recovered about 300 metres from the petrol pump . A bomb disposal squad of the Army reached in time and defused it .
