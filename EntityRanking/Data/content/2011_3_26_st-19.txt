Maoists blasted a state-run school at a village in Bihar 's Aurangabad district , the police said . Over 50 ultras surrounded the school building in Tetrain village in the Deo police station area and triggered a dynamite blast to blow it up on Thursday night , they said . Three rooms of the building were destroyed in the blast , they said . A powerful bomb was also recovered from the adjoining Yadupur village , where the Naxalites blew up a school building last year . Raids are on in the area to nab the ultras . PTI OPEN
