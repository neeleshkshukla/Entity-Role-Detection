A woman was seriously injured in Ariankuppam on Tuesday after a country bomb given to her in a package went off at her house . Jyothi ( 27 ) , a resident of Ariankuppam , received a call on her mobile phone asking her to come out of the house and collect a package . She went back inside and placed the parcel underneath her bed when it went off . Senior Superintendent of Police ( Law and Order ) V.J. Chandiran said that the woman was out of danger and being treated at a hospital . He said that the asbestos roof was ripped off in the impact of the blast . A case has been registered and a special team formed to investigate , Mr. Chandiran said .
