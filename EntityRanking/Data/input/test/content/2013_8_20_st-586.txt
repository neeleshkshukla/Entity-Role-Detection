Crude bombs were blasted by miscreants at corporation road area under town police station limits on Monday evening at around 7 p.m. . One person named Sheikh Shapu was injured . He was admitted in MKCG medical college and hospital in the city . According to sources a group of four to five miscreants were behind this bomb blast . Police officials including Berhampur Superintendent of Police ( SP ) rushed to the spot to investigate . One person has been rounded up by police in relation to this case .
