Simultaneous car bombings killed at least 38 people and left a trail of destruction in a town near Syria 's capital on Wednesday , as rebels downed a military aircraft for a second straight day . The explosives-packed cars were detonated at daybreak in a pro-regime neighbourhood of the mainly Christian and Druze town of Jaramana , residents , state media and a rights watchdog reported . The blasts ripped through a central square near a petrol station , sending residents fleeing in panic .
