Twenty-two persons were injured when a bomb planted on a cycle went off at a crowded place at Bijni in Chirang district at around 11-05 a.m. on Thursday . The injured have been admitted to the Lower Assam Hospital at Bongaigaon . Suspicion . Inspector-General of Police Law and Order Bhaskarjyoti Mahanta told The Hindu that the bomb was suspected to have been planted by the United Liberation Front of Asom ULFA . The recent arrests and surrender of its cadres might have been forced the ULFA to trigger the blast , he claimed .
