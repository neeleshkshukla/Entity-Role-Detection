Accused in the custody of the Special Investigation Team ( SIT ) of the Crime Branch CID Nazir ( 30 ) was subjected to an identification parade at Coimbatore Central Prison on Monday . He was arrested by the Border Security Force ( BSF ) along the Bangladesh border and was a wanted accused in bomb blast cases in Bangalore and Kerala , SIT sources said . He was also a wanted accused in the planting of a bomb at Coimbatore Press Club on December 30 , 2002 . The planting of the bomb was part of the campaign by the activists seeking the release of Kerala-based People 's Democratic Party ( PDP ) leader Abdul Nasir Maudhany . He was arrested and lodged at Coimbatore Central Prison as an undertrial prisoner in connection with the Coimbatore Serial Blasts Case . OPEN
