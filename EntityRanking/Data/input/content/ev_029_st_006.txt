New Delhi , May 26 ( UNI ) Prime Minister Manmohan Singh today strongly condemned the Guwahati bomb blast that left at least seven people dead . . . '' Such violent acts only harm the lives of ordinary people , '' Dr Singh said in a statement here . . . He sent his condolences to the families of those who lost their lives in the blast , triggered by ULFA militants in the busy Athgaon area this morning . . . Police said a bomb was placed in an auto-rickshaw hired by militants . . . .
