A man and his son were seriously injured when country-made bombs stored inside a motor room went off at Tharamangalam , near Omalur , on Saturday . They have been admitted to hospital . Ramasamy ( 67 ) of Karuvannur was trying to switch on the motor of his pump set on the field when the bombs , stocked by a manufacturer whose licence expired last month , exploded . Ramasamys son Periyasamy came to his fathers rescue and sustained injuries . The police said that a short circuit might have triggered the blast . OPEN
