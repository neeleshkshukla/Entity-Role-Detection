Kamal Chauhan , a disgruntled former Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh worker arrested by the National Investigation Agency , on Tuesday claimed that he had planted bombs on board the Samjhauta Express in 2007 . The blast left 68 people , mostly Pakistanis , dead . Yes , I have done it on my will , Chauhan told journalists as he was being taken out after in-camera proceedings in the Panchkula court , which granted the NIA his custody till February 24 for questioning him on his role in the blast on the Delhi-Lahore train .
