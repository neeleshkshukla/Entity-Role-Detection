The Police Commissioner of Delhi has announced a reward of Rs .25 lakh for any information leading to the arrest of the culprits of last year 's Jama Masjid bomb blast case here . A Maruti car had caught fire near Jama Masjid following a mild blast on September 19 . A few hours earlier , two young men on a motorcycle had opened fire at a group of foreigners . Also , the reward money for any information on the attack on the foreigners has been increased from Rs .10 lakh to Rs .30 lakh . OPEN
