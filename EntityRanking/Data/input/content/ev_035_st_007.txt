HYDERABAD : The CPI has condemned the killing of several people in bomb blasts by militants in Assam and demanded that necessary steps be taken for restoring peace in the troubled State . The partys State Executive which met here on Thursday expressed its shock over the heinous act and conveyed its condolences to the members of the bereaved families . It sought payment of Rs .5 lakh each to the kin of those killed in the blasts . Special Correspondent .
