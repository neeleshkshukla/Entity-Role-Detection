A special team of the Tamil Nadu and Karnataka police on Tuesday arrested a former Al-Umma activist , Kichan Buhari , here in connection with the April 17 Bangalore bomb blast . He hails from of Melapalayam in Tirunelveli . He is the third suspect to be held in the case . The team was shadowing Buhari from Coimbatore to Tirunelveli and nabbed him at Mattuthavani here at 7 a.m. , a police official said . The Karnataka police have taken him to Bangalore . Special Correspondent reports from Tirunelveli : Buharis mother Thowlath said her son was innocent . He is now leading a peaceful life . His children are studying in Coimbatore and he used to visit us twice a month , she said . OPEN
