A National Investigation Agency ( NIA ) team visited the Mecca Masjid here on Wednesday after taking up investigation into the bomb explosion case at the masjid four years ago in which nine persons were killed . Along with the Mecca Masjid case , the NIA had taken up a probe into two more bomb blast cases pertaining to Malegaon and Ajmer Sharief , in which the involvement of right-wing terror groups was suspected . The team had discussions with the city police which initially probed the Masjid blast case before handing it over to the CBI . OPEN
