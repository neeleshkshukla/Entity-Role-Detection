Over 100 Buddhist monks associated with the Mahabodhi Society of India staged a protest at Metro Channel in the Esplanade area here on Sunday condemning the serial blasts at Bodh Gaya in Bihar . Similar protests would be held in the next few days , said Anand Kumar Barua , a member of the Societys governing body . We had never imagined that such an incident will happen in the complex where Lord Buddha attainted enlightenment . Buddhism is a religion of peace . Such an incident goes against our faith and our belief in non-violence , Mr. Barua told The Hindu . Staff Reporter .
