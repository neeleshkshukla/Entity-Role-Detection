GUWAHATI : In a powerful explosion in Manipur eight people were killed and over 10 injured on Friday . . . Police sources told ET , `` Militants exploded powerful bomb Nagamapal area in Imphal West district of Manipur . The bomb was planted in a shop and explosion took place around 7.45 pm . The victims of the blast are believed to be non-Manipuri persons . '' . . Sources said no militant outfit has claimed the responsibility fort the blast . `` There are several militants ' outfits operating in the state . '' .
