A series of bomb blasts killed at least 21 people in the Indian holy city of Varanasi last night . . . Security forces across the country were on high alert as intelligence officials hinted that the explosions , which also injured more than 60 people , were the work of Islamic militants . . . Varanasi , 450 miles east of New Delhi , is one of Hinduism 's holiest cities and is filled with pilgrims visiting temples and bathing in the holy waters of the Ganges , which runs through the city . . . The first blast at the crowded Sankat Mochan temple killed 10 worshippers . Witnesses said debris , body parts and blood covered the temple 's floor . . . India factfile . . Eleven more people died in explosions 45 minutes later at a station a few miles away and inside a nearby train . A fourth bomb was defused in a residential district of Varanasi . . . `` The entire country is shocked by the blasts and is trying to cope with the situation , '' said Mulayam Singh Yadav , chief minister of the state of Uttar Pradesh .
