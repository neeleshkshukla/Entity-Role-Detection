Judge of the Special Court for Bomb Blast Cases Ganesan on Monday sentenced P. Venkatachalapathy to undergo life sentence in connection with the murder of his wife Hemambigai . According to prosecution , Hemambigai was hacked to death by the accused at Singanallur on March 14 , 2009 over a wordy duel while her mother sustained injuries . Investigating Officer in the case , Inspector , T.H. Ganesh arrested the accused . The Judge also ordered Venkatachalapathy to pay a fine of Rs . 10,000 and ten years Rigorous Imprisonment and a fine of Rs . 7,500 for the charge of attempt to murder .
