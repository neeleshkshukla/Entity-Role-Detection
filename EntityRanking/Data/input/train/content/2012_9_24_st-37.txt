Army personnel had a narrow escape on Sunday , when a powerful remote-controlled bomb exploded in Kwakeithel , near here , seconds after their truck crossed the place on the Imphal-Tiddim road . The bomb buried near a tree caused a foot-deep crater . A manhunt by the police shortly after the blast drew a blank . So far , no underground organisation has claimed responsibility . Police said the explosion was part of the coordinated violent campaign against the security forces by the United National Liberation Front and the Revolutionary Peoples Front .
