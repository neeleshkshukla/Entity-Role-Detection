Chairman of moderate Hurriyat faction Mirwaiz Umar Farooq on Thursday condemned the Mumbai serial blasts saying such incidents are orchestrated to derail the dialogue process between India and Pakistan . . . `` We are saddened by the huge loss of life in the Mumbai blasts and condemn it . Spilling the blood of innocents ? be it in Mumbai , Palestine , Karachi or Kashmir ? is a shameful and inhuman act , '' the Mirwaiz said in a statement .
