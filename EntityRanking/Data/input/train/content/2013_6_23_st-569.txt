A group of three miscreants tried to create havoc at Bauri Sahi square of Bijipur area in the city on Saturday morning by hurling a crude bomb and firing in the air . According to locals a group of three youths had reached the spot on a bike and searched for a youth . They also hurled a crude bomb which did not blast . When people of the area tried to nab the miscreants , they fired a round into the air and escaped from the spot . Police officials rushed to the spot . The live crude bomb and cartridge were seized .
