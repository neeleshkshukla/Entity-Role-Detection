Two youths belonging to Armoor town and Vannel ( B ) village of Balkonda mandal were killed in the suicide bomb blast in Kabul in Afghanistan on Tuesday . The victims were identified as Naveen ( 24 ) and Sandeep ( 24 ) . Naveen , after having completed his hotel management course , had gone to Kabul to work in a hotel , while Sandeep was doing some manual works . Naveen visited his family members in February last , while Sandeep got married six months ago . OPEN
