The family of Khalid Mujahid , an accused in a bomb blast case who died under mysterious circumstances in police custody , has refused to accept compensation offered by the Uttar Pradesh government . It refused to accept a cheque for Rs . 6 lakh from Horticulture Minister Paras Nath Yadav , who went to Khalids house at Marhiyahu in Jaunpur district on Wednesday . An accused in the Lucknow and Faizabad courts bomb blast case , Khalid died when he was being brought back to jail after a hearing in the Faizabad district court last fortnight . As his family alleged foul play , Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav referred the case to the Central Bureau of Investigation . OPEN
