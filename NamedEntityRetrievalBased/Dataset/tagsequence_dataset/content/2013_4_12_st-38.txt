Maoists blasted a building of the irrigation department in Aurangabad district of Bihar in the early hours of Thursday . The incident took place near Bigha village in Deo block on the Deo-Abma Road . More than 50 Maoists were involved , the police said . The building has seven structures , which were being repaired . Two were severely damaged and the remaining partially damaged , Superintendent of Police Daljeet Singh told The Hindu . The police recovered a live cane bomb and defused it , he said . A case under the Arms Act and the Unlawful Activities ( Prevention ) Act has been registered . OPEN
