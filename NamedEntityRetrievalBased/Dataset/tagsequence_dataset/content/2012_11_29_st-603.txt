Two youths were arrested by personnel of Baidyanathpur police station of the city on Wednesday in relation to the case of bombarding at Ankuli industrial estate area on November 26 . One person had been killed in the bomb blast . Two of the attackers had also been seriously injured when a bomb exploded in their hand by accident . These injured attackers were undergoing treatment in MKCG Medical College Hospital under police custody . According to police sources five persons were involved in that case of bombing . Only one of the accused was still absconding . OPEN
