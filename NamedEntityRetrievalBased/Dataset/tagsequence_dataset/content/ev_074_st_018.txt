NEW DELHI : In yet another shocking incident five persons were killed and four injured when a bomb exploded in the 2423A Dibrugarh-New Delhi Rajdhani Express in the wee hours of Thursday . . . According to sources , the bomb exploded at around 2 am near Golaghat in Assam when the blast occured . The train was between Nuajan and Chauan stations . Preliminary investigations suggest that the bomb was planted on the tracks . . . However , the exact number of casualties has not been known yet as the incident occurred in wee hours of Thursday morning and the toll may go up . . . Meanwhile , government machinery has rushed to the spot and assessing the situation . The railways have also stepped up operations to rescue the passengers stranded in the train .
