Two powerful bombs exploded at Ningombam in the Thoubal Assembly constituency , the stronghold of Manipur Chief Minister Okram Ibobi Singh , around midnight on Saturday night . However , there was no casualty in the blasts , alleged to be part of the continuing campaign by the CorCom ( Co-ordination Committee ) of seven proscribed underground organisations against Congress candidates . The CorCom of UNLF , the RPF , the KCP , the PREPAK , the PREPAK ( Pro ) , the KYKL and the UPPK had banned the Congress from contesting the Assembly elections , alleging that it was anti-people and anti- revolutionary . Besides , it played a major role in the merger of Manipur with Indian Union . OPEN
