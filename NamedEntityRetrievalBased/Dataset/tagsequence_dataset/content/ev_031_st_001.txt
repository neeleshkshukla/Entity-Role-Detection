Guwahati : One person was killed and 20 people were injured in a bomb blast suspected to have been triggered by the United Liberation Front of Asom in the Bamunimaidan locality here on Saturday . . . Police said a bomb kept in a three-wheeler went off at 11.45 a.m. at the Railway Colony market of Bamunimaidan , a busy commercial area , leading to the explosion of three gas cylinders in the shops nearby . Several shops were gutted . The windowpanes of nearby houses were shattered . . . The three-wheeler , in which the bomb was kept hidden , was blown to pieces . . . The vehicle had a sticker of the Indian airline on its body . Additional Superintendent of Police Rajen Singh said the police were trying to ascertain the facts . . . They said it was not immediately known whether the driver of the van was among the critically injured persons . . . The cylinder blasts caused panic . There was confusion over whether another blast had taken place . . . The deceased was identified as Ramlal Das -LRB- 60 -RRB- , a cobbler from Begusarai in Bihar . There were heart-rending scenes as the family members and relatives of the victims gathered at the site . The injured were taken to the Guwahati Medical College and Hospital . . . Assam Chief Minister Tarun Gogoi condemned the incident as an act of cowardice and asked the Director-General of Police R.N. Mathur to review the security measures in and around the city and other parts of the State .
