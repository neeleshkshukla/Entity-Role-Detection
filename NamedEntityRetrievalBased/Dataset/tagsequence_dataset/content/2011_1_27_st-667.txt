A primary school student was seriously injured when a crude bomb blasted near him at Gundhani village under Raikia police station in Kadhamal district . The injured child was Chandan Pradhan ( 8 ) , a student of class three in the local residential school for tribals and dalits . He accidentally stepped on a crude bomb hidden near a pond of the village , which blasted . He was admitted to the Raikia Community Health Centre ( CHC ) . OPEN
