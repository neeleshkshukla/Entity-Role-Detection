NEW DELHI : About 400 personnel of the Rapid action Force -LRB- RAF -RRB- have been rushed to Jaipur following the serial blasts in the pink city which claimed 60 lives and left 150 injured . ` We have rushed four companies of the RAF to the pink city after the blasts ' , CRPF spokesperson Ajay Chaturvedi said . Two of these companies are from Delhi and two from Aligargh , he said .
