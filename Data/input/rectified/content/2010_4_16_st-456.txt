Three bombs ripped through traditional New Year festivities in Myanmar 's biggest city of Yangon on Thursday , killing nine people and injuring 94 others , said officials and state television . There was no indication as to who was behind the blasts , which occurred at about 3 p.m. near some 20 pavilions erected for the celebrations at the sprawling Kandawgyi Lake . In the past , the government has blamed bomb blasts on exiled anti-government groups and insurgents .
