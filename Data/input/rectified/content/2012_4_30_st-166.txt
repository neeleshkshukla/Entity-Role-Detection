S. Sukannthan ( 27 ) who was injured along with his accomplice Sathya ( 32 ) while making country bombs early on Friday morning at Kilpauk lost six of his fingers in the explosion . Sukanthan was admitted to Stanley Medical College Hospital for treatment after the blast and was later shifted to the Royapettah GH . The doctors found he had lost four fingers on his right hand and two on his left when they removed his bandages .
