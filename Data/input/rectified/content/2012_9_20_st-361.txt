At least nine people were killed in a blast apparently targeting a vehicle of the Pakistan Air Force in Peshawar on Wednesday . Together with Tuesday twin blasts in Karachi aimed at the Bohra community , bombs have taken 16 lives in 24 hours . The Dawoodi Bohra community has so far never been targeted . Six of the seven killed in Karachi , were from the community . The attacks came a day after the visit of Syedi Mufaddal Bhaisaheb Saifuddin , the designated successor of Syedna Mohammad Burhanuddin . Dawn reported that a large number of Bohras from across the country and India were visiting Karachi in connection with the visit but no Indians were killed or hurt in the explosions .
