An explosion in a bus carrying employees of the state secretariat of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa ( KPK ) government on Friday afternoon killed at least 19 of them and injured 45 , according to Shiraz Paracha , spokesperson for the KPK Chief Minister . This comes after Sundays Peshawar church blast that claimed 80 lives . Police said a timer device was used to set off the bomb which could have been placed in a bag in the bus . Mr. Paracha told The Hindu on the phone that the bus was ferrying more than 60 government employees back home when the incident happened , 15 km away from Peshawar .
