The Central Crime Branch picked up the 11th suspect in connection with the April 17 Malleswaram bomb blast case , from Madurai prison on Saturday . Shaleen ( 35 ) was picked up by the police along with Kitchen Buhari on April 22 and let off the next day . He was then picked up by the Tamil Nadu police in connection with the attack on a local BJP worker and handed over to the Nesarmani police in Nagercoil town where he was remanded in judicial custody and lodged in the Madurai prison . On Saturday , a team of police picked him up to question his role in the blast . Shaleen was brought to Bangalore and produced before the 1st Additional Chief Metropolitan Magistrate and taken into 14 days custody . The police also produced Kitchen Buhari and Basheer and got four days custody .
