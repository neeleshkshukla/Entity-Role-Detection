The National Investigation Agency ( NIA ) Special Court on Wednesday declared two accused , Mohammed Azar and P.P. Usman , as proclaimed offenders ' in the Kozhikode twin bomb blast cases . The court made the proclamation on a petition filed by the NIA seeking to make them proclaimed offenders ' as the agen cy 's efforts to trace them had come to naught . Meanwhile , the court extended the judicial remand of T. Nazir and other accused in the case .
