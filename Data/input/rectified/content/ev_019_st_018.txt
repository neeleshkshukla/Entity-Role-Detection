Days after the blast in West Bengals Bardhaman district where two persons died , the police seized a large quantity of gelatine sticks and detonators from Raghunathpur area in Purulia district on Saturday . Two persons have been detained . Acting on a tip off we intercepted a vehicle and seized 2,700 gelatine sticks and 11,000 detonators , Purulia Superintendent of Police S. K. Neelakantam told The Hindu .
