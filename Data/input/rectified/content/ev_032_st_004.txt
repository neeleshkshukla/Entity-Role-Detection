Two persons were killed and 18 injured as three serial blasts , suspected to have been triggered by ULFA , rocked Assam on Sunday . One person was killed and 16 injured in a car bomb explosion at Bengpukhuri area in Tinsukia town on Sunday evening , officials said . In another blast in Athgaon area here , one person was killed and two were injured . The third explosion took place in Fatasil Ambari area of the city but there was no report of any casualty . .
