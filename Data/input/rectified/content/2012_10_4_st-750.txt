At least 40 people were killed and 90 wounded , most of them soldiers , when three car bombs struck the heart of Syria 's second city Aleppo on Wednesday , a monitoring group said . A medical source said that at least 40 people were killed and 90 injured , said the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights . Most of them were regime troops . Meanwhile , official television channel Al-Ikhbariya said 31 people were killed and dozens wounded . The toll could rise because many people were badly injured , a city official told AFP , shortly after three bombs exploded around Saadallah al-Jabiri Square , near a military officers ' club and a hotel . Two of the blasts hit the area near the club within a minute of each other . A third car bomb then exploded some 150 metres way in the Bab Jnein district , at the entrance of of Aleppo .
