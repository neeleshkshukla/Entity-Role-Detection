Officials of the Special Enquiry Squad of Central Crime Branch ( CCB ) interrogated Shahzad , an alleged Indian Mujahideen militant , in connection with the April 17 Chinnaswamy Stadium blasts , here on Friday . Shahzad and his associate Salman were brought here from Delhi on Thursday . The CCB officials produced the two in the first ACMM Court which allowed the police to interrogate them till October 30 . A team headed by an Assistant Commissioner of Police interrogated Shahzad on Friday while Salman will be questioned on Saturday , a senior police officer , who wished to remain anonymous , told The Hindu . Shahzad was grilled about his role in the crude bomb blasts ahead of an IPL match outside the cricket stadium and his alleged links with banned terrorist organisation , Indian Mujahideen .
