One person died when a group of miscreants hurled crude bombs at him near the Jagannath temple in Ankuli industrial estate area of the city on Monday evening . The deceased was identified as Bauribandhu Choudhury . He was standing at spot when at around 6.30 p.m. when the attackers blasted bombs targeting him . Two other youths were also injured . According to the police , the injured persons were suspected to be from the attacker group and were injured when accidentally a bomb blasted in their hands .
