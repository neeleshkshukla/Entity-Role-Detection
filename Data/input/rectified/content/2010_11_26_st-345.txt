The police have released a sketch of a person suspected to be behind the bomb explosion at Veliyam on the night of November 19 . The police said the man was seen moving around in suspicious circumstances and vanished soon after the blast . The sketch was prepared on the basis of information given by those who claimed to have the person . On the basis of eyewitness accounts , the police said the suspect would be about 22 years of age , roughly 5.4 ft height with dark complexion . Information about the person can be passed on to the Punalur Dy . SP at 0475-2222595 or 9497990026 .
