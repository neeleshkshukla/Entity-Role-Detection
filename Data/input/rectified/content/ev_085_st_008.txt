The Popular Front of India -LRB- PFI -RRB- has strongly condemned the bomb blast which took place in Varanasi on December 7 . . . In a press release here on Friday , PFI Udupi district unit president Niyamath Ali said that the blast was a senseless and a shameful act . It was done with the purpose for creating terror among people and disturbing inter-community relations . . . Such acts were intended to weaken the people 's united struggle for justice on the Babri Masjid issue .
