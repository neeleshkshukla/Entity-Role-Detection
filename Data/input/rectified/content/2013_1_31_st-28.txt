The National Investigation Agency arrested Dileep Jagpat , 42 , suspected to have been involved in the 2007 Samjhauta Express blast and the 2006 Malegaon bombing , at Kishanganj on Wednesday . Dileep Jagpat was arrested from Kishanganj area of the district , sources said . The NIA team is camping in western Madhya Pradesh to probe several blast cases , they said .
