National ULFA triggers two blasts in Assam . . By Sushanta Talukdar . . GUWAHATI , APRIL 3 . The United Liberation Front of Asom has triggered two more blasts since Saturday night , even as police today recovered five explosive devices from different parts of Assam . A panchayat office was badly damaged in a blast suspected to have been triggered by the militant outfit in Dhemaji district late last night . Another blast occurred in upper Assam 's Jorhat town tonight when militants lobbed a grenade . A major tragedy was averted when a device recovered near the blast site was defused by explosive experts . Police also recovered an explosive device in Tinsukia and three crude bombs in Dhubri district .
