National Kalam condemns blasts . . Special Correspondent . . NEW DELHI : President Abdul Kalam has condemned the serial blasts that rocked the capital on Saturday evening and condoled the loss of lives in the explosions . ` Dr Kalam has appealed to the people to maintain calm and help in relief and rescue operations , '' S. M. Khan , Press Adviser to the President said . . . The President spoke to Home Minister Shivraj Patil and Delhi Chief Minister Sheila Dikshit and took stock of the relief work being undertaken by the authorities . . . Vice President Bhairon Singh Shekhawat also expressed his grief and concern over the bomb blasts . . . Former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee condemned the blast and appealed to the people to remain calm and united in this hour of crisis .
