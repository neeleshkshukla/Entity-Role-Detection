The Q Branch CID of the State Intelligence on Friday arrested two persons alleged to be former LTTE cadres . Acting on a tip-off , a special team apprehended Sivanesan and Maheswaran alias Gopi , both Sri Lankan nationals , here . Agency sources said the duo was part of a gang that conspired to trigger bomb blasts in Sri Lanka . They carried out trial blasts at a remote location in Cuddalore districts in December 2012 .
