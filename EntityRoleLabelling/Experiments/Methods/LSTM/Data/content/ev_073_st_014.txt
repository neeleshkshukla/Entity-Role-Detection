Terror struck Uttar Pradesh on Friday when militants triggered near-simultaneous blasts in court premises in Varanasi , Faizabad and Lucknow killing 15 people , four of them lawyers , and injuring over 80 . . . Six bombs -- three in Varansi , two in Faizabad and one in Lucknow -- some planted on cycles , went off within a span of 15 minutes in the crowded court complexes between 1310 hours and 1325 hours . . . Ammonium Nitrate and timer devices were used in triggering the blasts in Lucknow and Varanasi , sources said . . .
