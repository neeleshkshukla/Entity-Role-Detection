Peoples Democratic Party vice-chairman Subair Sabahi has urged the State government to take necessary action for the release of party chairman Abdul Nasir Maudany from jail where he is kept as an undertrial in the Bangalore bomb blast case . PDP has decided to launch a Statewide agitation for Maudanys release . A convention will be held at Kozhikode Town Hall on January 25 . Special Correspondent OPEN
