Nalbari , ( Assam ) , Nov 22 ( ANI ) : Just four days before the first anniversary of the 26/11 terror attacks , bomb blasts in Assam claimed the lives of five people and injured about 30 . . . All the three-bomb blast took place in Assam 's Nalbari District . . . At around 10.30 in the morning two of the explosions occurred outside a police station in Nalbari , which is around 70 kilometers from Guwahati . . . A third bomb blast took place in the Gopal Bazar area . . . Further details are awaited . No outfit has claimed responsibility for the blast . ( ANI ) . . .
