Guwahati : Seven persons were injured , two of them critically , in a car bomb explosion suspected to have been triggered by the United Liberation Front of Asom -LRB- ULFA -RRB- in upper Assam 's Tinsukia town on Friday . . . Police said that the blast occurred on the busy Chambers Road in Tinsukia when a bomb hidden in a Maruti gypsy vehicle went off around 7 . 20 a.m. . . The police arrested five persons in this connection with the blast . . . Earlier on Thursday night three suspected ULFA militants were killed in an encounter with the Army in lower Assam 's Nalbari district . The encounter took place at Barkhanajan , about 3 km of Nalbari town .
