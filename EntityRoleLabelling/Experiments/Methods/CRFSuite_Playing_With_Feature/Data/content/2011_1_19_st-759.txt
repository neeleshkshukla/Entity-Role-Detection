An eight-year-old girl was killed and 12 people were injured , five seriously , in a bomb blast at Kazirganj Bazar in Bihar 's Nawada district on Tuesday . An unidentified cycle-borne person threw the bomb at a fish shop in the market which exploded killing the girl on the spot , SubDivisional Officer S Z Hasan said . The injured were admitted to Sadar hospital where the condition of five was stated to be critical , he said . OPEN
