The National Investigation Agency ( NIA ) has taken Rajendra Chowdhary alias Samundar into custody for interrogation in connection with the 2007 Mecca Masjid bomb blast . The NIA had filed a prisoners transit warrant in a Haryana court and brought him to the city a few days ago . He was remanded to judicial custody by the magistrate . The NIA later filed a plea seeking cu-stody of Chowdhary . After hearing the arguments , the court granted police custody of Chowdhary till March 26 . Chowdhary may be taken to various places in the country to corroborate facts pertaining to the case , sources said . He had been lodged in a Haryana jail for his alleged involvement in Samjhauta Express blast . OPEN
