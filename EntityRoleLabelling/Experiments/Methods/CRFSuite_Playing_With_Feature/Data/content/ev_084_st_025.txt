THIRUVANANTHAPURAM : In the wake of the Pune bomb blast and Maoist attack at Silda in West Bengal , Kerala chief minister V S Achuthanandan said strong steps would be taken to safeguard the state from subversive activities of anti-national forces . ` Terrorist strikes being reported from different parts of the country are matters of grave concern , ' he said . Terrorist threat should be viewed seriously and stern measures taken to tackle the menace , Achuthanandan told reporters here after a state cabinet meeting The incidents of blast triggered by terrorist at Pune claiming 11 lives and Maoist attack on the Eastern Frontier Rifles ' camp at Silda in which 24 jawans were gunned down were deplorable , he said .
