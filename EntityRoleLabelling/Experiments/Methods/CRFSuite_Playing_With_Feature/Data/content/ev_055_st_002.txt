At least 10 persons were injured tonight when a bomb exploded in a New Delhi-bound train from Bareilly in Moradabad district of Uttar Pradesh , a Northern Railway spokesman said . . . The blast took place at around 8.05 p.m. in a sleeper class compartment of the Bareilly-New Delhi passenger train which was at the Chandausi station . The condition of one of the injured was serious , the spokesman added . . . The coach in which the explosion took place was detached from the train before it resumed its onward journey . Senior railway officials rushed to the site with relief and rescue teams . PTI .
