Union Home Minister Sushil Kumar Shinde on Thursday ruled out a fresh probe into the 2010 Pune German Bakery blast case . His statement comes after Indian Mujahideen co-founder Yasin Bhatkal reportedly told his interrogators that he was not accompanied by Pune blast convict Mirza Himayat Baig in planting the bomb in the bakery , but by another terrorist , Qateel Siddiqui . Baig has been awarded death sentence in connection with the blast that claimed 17 lives and left over 60 people injured . PTI OPEN
