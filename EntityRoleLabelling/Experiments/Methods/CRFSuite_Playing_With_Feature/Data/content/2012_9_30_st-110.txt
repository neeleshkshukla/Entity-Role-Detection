: One person was injured when a bomb planted near the steps of a TASMAC shop on Theni-Cumbum Main Road went off here on Saturday night . The injured Sivakumar ( 33 ) of Allinagaram was admitted to Theni Government Medical College Hospital . Many persons near the shop reportedly received minor injuries . But no one informed about the injuries so far . The blast spot was littered with damaged wires and battery parts and pieces of ropes . With no smell from explosives , the police suspected that the bomb could be operated with electronically operated device . The bomb squad members collected samples from the blast site . OPEN
