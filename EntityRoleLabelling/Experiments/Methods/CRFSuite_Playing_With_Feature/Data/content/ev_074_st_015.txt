GUWAHATI : Five people were killed and four others injured in a bomb blast in a Delhi-bound Rajdhani Express in Assam 's Golaghat district early on Thursday morning . . . The explosion , carried out by suspected militants , happened in the luggage van of the 2423A Dibrugarh-Guwahati-Delhi Rajdhani between Naujan and Sugajan at around 1 am , North Eastern railway CPRO Trikal Rabha said . . . Five people died on the spot while the four injured were rushed to Golaghat Civil hospital . The luggage van was damaged as well as small portion of track . . . The train resumed its journey at around 6am after passengers in the affected van were shifted to a 3 tier AC boggie . Top railway officials have rushed to the site .
