A trooper of 31 Assam Rifles was injured in a bomb blast triggered by militants at Saikhong village in Churachandpur district of Manipur on Sunday . He was on foot patrol with some others . In another incident , a 12-year-old boy was injured when a bomb went off while it was being removed by the police on Highway 39 at Lilong in Thoubal district . A grenade was hurled at the camp of 116 Bn Border Security Force at Heirok in Thoubal district . In a statement , the Revolutionary Peoples Front said its military wing , the Peoples Liberation Army , was behind the bomb attacks . The Imphal West district police arrested Ibomcha Gurumayum ( 52 ) , said to be a member of the Peoples Revolutionary Party of Kangleipak ( Pro group ) , from Kwakeithel Heinou Khongnembi . Eight hand grenades were seized from him . OPEN
