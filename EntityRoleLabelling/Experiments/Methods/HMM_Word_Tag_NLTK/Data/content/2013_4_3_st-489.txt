Family members of six of the 17 persons killed in the Dilsukhnagar twin bomb blasts were handed over ex-gratia of Rs . 1 lakh each by Mayor Mohd . Majid Hussain at the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation ( GHMC ) head office on Tuesday . Ex-gratia in phases Deputy Mayor Rajkumar and GHMC Commissioner M.T. Krishna Babu were also present when the cheques were issued to the families of V. Ramulu , Swapna Reddy , Mohd . Rafiuddin , N. Venkateswarlu , Aijaz Ahmed and Ahmedullah Khan . Ex-gratia for the rest too would be given in phases , an official spokesperson of the GHMC said . OPEN
