New Delhi : The Jamaat-e-Islami Hind has demanded ` stringent action ' against those who carried out a string of bomb attacks in Patna killing six people . Expressing grief over the deaths , Jamaat secretary general Nusrat Ali warned that communal forces might now create an environment of ` fear and terror ' in Bihar for electoral gains . . . Ali urged the Bihar and the central governments to maintain peace ahead of the Lok Sabha election due next year .
