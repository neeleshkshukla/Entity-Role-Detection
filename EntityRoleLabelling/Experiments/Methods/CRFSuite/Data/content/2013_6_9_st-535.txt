A bomb was blasted targeting a house at Harijan Street of Gosaninuagaon area of Berhampur on Saturday . According to Gosaninuagaon police , three persons of the targeted family including two women received minor injuries . As per eyewitnesses , two youths hurled bomb at the backdoor of home of Upendra Hati at around 11 a.m. . Past rivalry between neighbours was suspected to be the reason behind this incident , police sources said . OPEN
