Bodh Gaya : A day after multiple blasts , regular prayers were organised at the Mahabodhi temple in Bodh Gaya . Earlier , the Bihar Police released the CCTV footage of one of the blasts . They have also detained a man named Vinod Mistry , whose identity card was found at the blast site , for questioning . Chief Minister Nitish Kumar said he will ask the Centre to appoint the Central Industrial Security Forces -LRB- CISF -RRB- to guard the world-famous temple .
