Three persons were injured in a grenade blast in upper Assam 's Tinsukia town on Monday evening . The condition of one of them is stated to be critical . . . Police said that two unidentified youths reportedly lobbed a grenade in the busy G.N.B. Road in the heart of Tinsukia town at 6.05 p.m. and sped away in a Maruti van . The glass panes of several vehicles and shops were smashed in the impact . No outfit has so far claimed responsibility . . . In another incident , security forces recovered a powerful bomb weighing about 5 kg in upper Assam 's Chabua town earlier in the day . A woman , who is suspected to have been the courier , has been taken into custody . . . Initial investigation revealed that bomb was brought from Kakopathar area and was being taken to Sivasagar town .
