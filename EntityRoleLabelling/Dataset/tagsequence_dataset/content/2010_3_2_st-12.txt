New Delhi : In the wake of Fridays bomb blasts in Kabul , India has sent a team of investigators to the Afghan capital . National Security Advisor Shiv Shankar Menon will pay a visit there on March 5 to discuss the security of Indians in Afghanistan . The team , including officials of Home and Defence Ministries , has joined its Afghan counterpart in probing the attack in which Indians engaged in developmental and reconstruction works in that country were targeted . Afghanistan has set up a five-member team to investigate the incident . The investigators suspect the involvement of the Taliban , particularly the Haqqani group , in league with elements of the Lashkar-e-Taiba in the assault . Mr. Menon is expected to meet President Hamid Karzai and other top Afghan leaders . PTI OPEN
