A suicide car bombing at a U.S. military base near a flashpoint city in eastern Afghanistan killed at least three Afghans and wounded seven others on Wednesday , officials said . The blast took place at the entrance of Forward Operating Base Chapman in Khost . Taliban claims responsibility The Taliban claimed responsibility for the attack . Taliban spokesman Zabihullah Mujahed said the attacker `` detonated a car bomb while American invading forces were searching visitors going to the base '' . `` One police officer who tried to search the vehicle and two civilians nearby were killed , '' he said . Khost province borders Pakistan , which is widely believed to be a key source of fighters , funds and supplies for the Taliban . The Haqqani network , a militant group close to Al-Qaeda and blamed for some of the most daring insurgent attacks in Afghanistan , is particularly active in the province.AFP OPEN
