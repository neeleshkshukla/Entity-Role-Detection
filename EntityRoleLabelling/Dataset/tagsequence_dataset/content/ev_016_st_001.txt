Front Page Bombs explode at Guwahati parade ground . . By Sushanta Talukdar . . GUWAHATI , JAN. 26 . Violence rocked Assam and Manipur on Republic Day as militants triggered blasts at the Parade Ground here injuring two Government employees and seven at Jiribam in Manipur . A sub-inspector and a Government employee were injured when two programmed time delay devices went off at Judges Field , venue of the ceremonial parade at Guwahati . The first one went off at 8.47 a.m. in a drain and the other at 9.05 a.m. . . The first blast occurred minutes after the Assam Chief Minister , Tarun Gogoi , arrived at the Parade Ground and the second when the Governor , Lt. Gen. -LRB- retd . -RRB- Ajai Singh , was getting ready for inspecting the guard of honour . . . Two persons were injured when a bomb , suspected to have been planted by the ULFA , went off near the broad gauge railway yard at Bamunimaidan around 7.10 p.m. . Yet another blast occurred at Bongaigaon town .
